About Stride Habits

Stride Habits is a “good vibes” habit tracker built primarily as a university project. It focuses on:

  • Creating simple recurring habits and routines.
  • Tracking completions and streaks without social feeds.
  • Keeping attached images on your device rather than in the cloud.

Text-based habit data may be synced to a small backend (Summit API) while the project is active. When the project ends, that backend – and its stored data – is planned to be shut down.

The app is provided “as is”, without guarantees, and is not a medical or mental-health tool. Always consult professionals for health-related questions.
